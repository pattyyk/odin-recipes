Brownies by Joshua Weissman

adapted by pk

Description

Originally made by Joshua Weissman, but I found it a bit too sweet, so I adjusted the sweetness according to my taste :)

Ingredients

Brownie batter

  • 170 g melted, unsalted butter
  • 210 g granulated sugar
  • 80 g cocoa powder
  • 73 g all-purpose flour
  • 3 g salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 g vanilla extract
  • 113 g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, cocoa powder, sea salt and espresso powder and set aside.
  2. In medium large bowl add sugar, melted unsalted butter, room temperature egg one at a time, and 1 tbsp (10 g) vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour mixture with the wet ingredients until nice, smooth and glossy.
  4. Fold in dark chocolate.
  5. Add mixture to greased parchment lined 8x8 baking pan
  6. Bake at 350 degrees F, 175 C 35-40 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool to room temp before cutting.
  7. Use a metal pan over glass when baking to make cutting friendlier.
  8. Top with flakey salt.
